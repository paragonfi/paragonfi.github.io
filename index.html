We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) Nigeria AML/CFT/CPF Regulations 2022.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Services:

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Identity and Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, nationality, residential address, and government-issued identification (e.g., international passport, national ID) for KYC/AML compliance.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, payment information, transaction history, and investment preferences, as required for brokerage services.
  • Profile Data: Username, password, security questions, and preferences for using the App.
  • KYC/AML Data: Information required for Customer Due Diligence (CDD), such as proof of address, source of funds, and beneficial ownership details, in compliance with SEC Nigeria AML/CFT/CPF Regulations 2022.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We collect certain information automatically when you use the App:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the App, and interactions with AI-driven insights.
  • Device Data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address or precise location (if you enable location services), used for fraud prevention and compliance with jurisdictional requirements.
  • Log Data: Error logs, crash reports, and performance data to improve the App's functionality.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third parties, such as:

  • Partners: Information from ARM Securities Limited, our sponsoring broker, including KYC/AML verification data and transaction records.
  • Regulatory Authorities: Data from the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU), or SEC Nigeria, as required for compliance.
  • Analytics Providers: Usage statistics from analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand user behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Process transactions, manage your account, and facilitate investments through ARM Securities Limited.
  • Compliance with Legal Obligations: Perform KYC/AML checks, report suspicious transactions to NFIU within 24 hours, and maintain records for at least 5 years, as required by SEC Nigeria AML/CFT/CPF Regulations 2022 (Regulations 21, 24).
  • AI-Driven Insights: Use AI to analyze market trends and user data to provide insights and analytics. These insights are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice (see Section 8).
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage data to enhance functionality, fix bugs, and improve user experience.
  • Communicate with You: Send account updates, transaction confirmations, security alerts, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Fraud Prevention: Monitor transactions and user activity to detect and prevent fraud, money laundering, terrorism financing, and proliferation financing, in compliance with SEC regulations.
  • Legal and Regulatory Purposes: Respond to requests from regulators (e.g., SEC, NFIU) or law enforcement, and comply with applicable laws.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • ARM Securities Limited: As our sponsoring broker, ARM receives necessary data (e.g., KYC/AML data, transaction details) to facilitate brokerage services and comply with SEC regulations.
  • Regulatory Authorities: We share information with SEC Nigeria, NFIU, and other competent authorities as required by law, including for AML/CFT/CPF compliance (Regulation 5).
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., cloud storage providers, payment processors, analytics providers) who assist us in operating the App, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Law Enforcement: In response to legal requests, court orders, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, with notice to you.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

ParagonFi uses artificial intelligence (AI) to provide insights, analytics, and market trends based on aggregated user data and publicly available information. These AI-driven insights are intended for informational purposes only and are not personalized financial advice, investment recommendations, or guarantees of future performance.

  • Limitations: AI insights are based on historical data and algorithms, which may not predict future outcomes accurately. We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of AI-generated insights.
  • No Liability for AI Insights: ParagonFi is not liable for any decisions, losses, or damages resulting from your reliance on AI-driven insights. You are solely responsible for your investment decisions (see Section 8).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest (e.g., SSL/TLS for data transmission).
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Secure storage of KYC/AML records in compliance with SEC Nigeria requirements.

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law (e.g., within 72 hours under GDPR).

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes:

  • KYC/AML Records: Retained for at least 5 years after the termination of your account or transaction, as required by SEC Nigeria AML/CFT/CPF Regulations 2022 (Regulation 21).
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes, unless required longer for legal reasons.
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 5 years after closure, unless you request deletion (subject to legal requirements).

After the retention period, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information, subject to applicable laws:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes (e.g., SEC compliance).
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances (e.g., while we verify a correction request).
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format (GDPR/CCPA).
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing communications via the link in emails or by contacting us.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where we rely on your consent (e.g., location data), though this may limit certain features.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@paragonfi.co. We will respond within 30 days (or 72 hours for NDPR requests). We may require identity verification to process your request.

CCPA Notice for California Residents: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data) and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.
